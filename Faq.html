Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. You can return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or through the carrier's tracking system.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination.

How can I contact customer service?

You can contact our customer service team through our contact page or by emailing Lollipop.Club@gmail.com. We strive to respond within 24 hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and Payments are securely processed through our website.
